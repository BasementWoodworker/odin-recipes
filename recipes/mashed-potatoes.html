Mashed Potatoes

Description

Quick and easy recipe. All you need is a pot and some potatoes

Ingridients:

  • potatoes
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tbsp butter

Steps:

  1. Peel your potatoes
  2. Put them in the pot and fill it with water 10mm above the potatoes. Add salt.
  3. Boil the potatoes until they are soft. You can check their readiness by poking them with a fork. They should feel soft and malleable.
  4. Pour away the water, leaving only a small amount (around 50ml)
  5. Mash the potatoes. If they look dry, add more warm water and mash them again. Repeat until the desired consistency is achieved.
  6. Add 1/2 tbsp of butter and mix again
